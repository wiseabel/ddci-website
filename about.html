About DDCI

The Dry Corridor Initiative (DDCI) is a community-led restoration program focused on greening and rehabilitating the cattle corridor regions of Uganda. We work with pastoral and agro-pastoral households in Kiruhura, Lyantonde, Ssembabule, Nakaseke, Luweero, and Nakasongola to plant resilient tree species, restore grazing lands, and build local capacity for sustainable land management.

Our Objectives

  1. Restore vegetation cover across targeted farm plots and communal grazing areas.
  2. Improve water retention and soil stability to increase pasture availability.
  3. Support farmers with seedlings, training, and long-term monitoring.
  4. Promote income-generating tree products and sustainable grazing practices.

How We Work

Our model combines:

  • Community mobilization and stakeholder engagement
  • Farmer training in tree nursery management and agroforestry
  • Seedling distribution and site preparation
  • Periodic monitoring and adaptive management
